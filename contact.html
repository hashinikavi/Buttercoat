Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How much advance notice do you need for orders?

We recommend at least 24 hours in advance. For custom or large orders, please provide 3-5 days notice.

❓ Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We deliver within 5 km of our location. Delivery charge starts at ₹50 depending on distance.

❓ Can I customize my cake?

Absolutely! We offer custom flavors, designs, and dietary options. Contact us with your requirements.

❓ What's your refund policy?

We guarantee fresh products. If unsatisfied, contact us within 2 hours of delivery for resolution.

❓ Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes! We have vegan, eggless, gluten-free, and sugar-free options. Please mention your requirements when ordering.

❓ What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, online transfers (UPI, bank transfer), and digital wallets. 50% advance is required for custom orders.
